## Scope & Context

When using many-to-many relationships, the junction objects appear in the sidebar which results in what is effectively clutter if the junction objects are only used for managing many-to-many relationships.

## Current behavior

E.g. in this case I have People who drive vehicles, with a Vehicle drivers junction object binding them together.
